Dry Skin

Description: Dry skin lacks adequate moisture, leading to a rough, flaky, or even cracked surface. It may feel tight, especially after cleansing, and can appear dull. Environmental factors like cold weather, low humidity, and harsh skincare products can worsen dryness. People with dry skin need to focus on hydrating and nourishing their skin to restore its natural barrier and retain moisture.

Benefits of Proper Care:

  • Improves skin hydration, making it feel soft, smooth, and comfortable.
  • Helps strengthen the skin barrier, reducing moisture loss and protecting against environmental irritants.
  • Prevents flakiness and reduces signs of irritation, such as redness and itchiness.
  • Promotes a radiant, healthy glow by keeping the skin well-nourished and hydrated.
