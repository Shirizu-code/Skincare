Oily Skin

Description: Oily skin is caused by an overproduction of sebum, the natural oil our skin produces. While sebum helps keep skin hydrated and protected, an excess can lead to a shiny or greasy appearance, especially on the forehead, nose, and chin. Individuals with oily skin often experience issues like acne, blackheads, and visibly enlarged pores. This skin type may be challenging to manage, but with the right skincare routine, it can look healthy and balanced.

Benefits of Proper Care:

  • Controls excess oil, helping skin appear less shiny and more matte.
  • Minimizes the appearance of enlarged pores, giving the skin a smoother texture.
  • Prevents breakouts and reduces the risk of acne by keeping pores clear.
  • Improves overall skin tone and texture for a healthier, fresher look.
