Cleanser

Description: A cleanser is a skincare product designed to remove dirt, oil, and makeup from the skin. Cleansing is an essential step in any skincare routine, as it helps to clear the skin of impurities, allowing it to breathe and prepare for subsequent treatments.

Benefits of Proper Cleansing:

  • Removes excess oil and impurities, preventing clogged pores.
  • Helps to maintain a clear and balanced complexion.
  • Prepares the skin for better absorption of skincare products.
  • Promotes a fresh and rejuvenated appearance.
